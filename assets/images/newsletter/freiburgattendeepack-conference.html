1. Timings & Registration

On Monday the 10th, we’ll open the doors and will be ready to welcome you at 8:30am. Registration will remain open all day, so don’t worry about dropping in a little later, but we hope you can join us for the big opening at 9:30AM.

You don’t need to print anything! Just present your photo ID, and we’ll have your badge ready and waiting for collection. After you'll have registered, that’s it! You’re good to go for the whole event — just please remember to keep hold of your badge as it gets you entry on Tuesday morning, as well as being your entry pass to our party.

The Schedule At A Glance

  • Sunday, September 9th:

    Pre-conf Warm Up Party: On Sunday, we hope you will join us for a drink, a lightning talk and panel with some of our speakers at SmashingConf Warm-up Party at Grünhof. We start at 7 PM, our doors open at 5:30 PM. Be sure to RSVP so we know you're coming! Please note this is a different venue from the rest of the SmashingConf week.

  • Monday, September 9th:

2. Pre-conf Warm Up Party at Grünhof

On Sunday, September 9th at 6 PM, just before the two main conference days, we’re happy to host a pre-conference warm-up party for SmashingConf attendees! This evening, held at the Grünhof, will be an evening of drinks, collaboration, and lightning talks! You can find the programme on our Meetup page.

The venue is located only 6 minutes walking from the bus and train station. If you're coming by car, there are a few parking lots nearby, but traffic can be quite slow-going, so be sure to plan accordingly!


Grünhof

Share some drinks, your knowledge, and experience! Please let us know if you intend to come! The capacity at the venue is limited so sign up in advance and bring your ID!

3. Freiburg Collaborative Google Doc!

Now imagine you arrived at the conference with everything prepared. Here comes the best part — learning and sharing with each other. But, what if we all collect interesting thoughts, techniques, and key takeaways from the conference in a shared Google Doc?

For this purpose, we've created a collaborative Google Doc for each day at Smashed.by/day01 and Smashed.by/day2. During the conference, we can all take notes together, resulting in one packed document with everyone's collaborative notes!

There is only the schedule and talk descriptions there now, but feel free to add your questions for speakers, so we can address them in the Q&A after the talk!


— Amanda, our resident AV nerd (and an awesome roller derby skater!).
