3. Accessibility Statement

SmashingConf is committed to providing an inclusive, accessible, and safe space for all attendees.

The Conference and Workshops will be held at Historical Merchants' Hall (Historisches Kaufhaus) a beautiful, wheelchair accessible, historic building located at Münsterplatz 24, 79098 Freiburg im Breisgau.

What To Expect

  • All talks will be in English.
  • Dietary requirement-compliant, healthy, nutritious food will be served throughout the day.
  • Snacks (fruits) and drinks (coffee, tea, water) will be available at all times.
  • Wi-Fi (pw: icecream) and power outlets will be available.
  • For the conference, the lobby and theater areas are flat, located on the second floor, with a lift available. We are happy to reserve seats for those with mobility restrictions.
  • Anyone with mobility, hearing or vision concerns can reserve seats.
  • Our bathrooms are multi-stall and handicap accessible.
  • Service dogs are welcome.
  • The party will be after the 1st conference day, at the conference venue, and we will be providing alcoholic beverages as well as soft drinks.
  • A Code of Conduct team is available throughout the entire event and parties to address any incidents, microaggressions or ableisms.
  • Our speakers have been advised to avoid using epilepsy-inducing videos and animations, however we cannot fully guarantee compliance.

4. Travel Tips

When to fly? Keep in mind that pre-conference activities start on Sunday, Sep 10th, with the Pre-conf Warm Up Party that night, so you might want to arrive a day early. The last conference talk will end at 6PM on Tuesday, Sep 12th. Also, there are workshops on Sunday and Wednesday, so be sure to plan accordingly!

Where to fly? Freiburg doesn’t have a dedicated airport, so you have a few options:

  • Fly to EuroAirport in Basel, Switzerland. It's 60km away and has many great (and affordable) Easyjet connections. From there, you take the Flixbus, for approx 50 minutes to your final desitination of "Freiburg Hauptbahnhof" (Freiburg Train Station), which is only 15-minute walk to the venue!
  • Fly to Zürich, Switzerland and then take the FlexiBus for approx 2 hours to "Freiburg Hauptbahnhof" (Freiburg Train Station).
  • Fly to Frankfurt Airport — the biggest airport in Germany, with hundreds of direct international flights available. From there ("Frankfurt Flughafen Fernbahnhof"), you can take the train for 2 hours to "Freiburg Hauptbahnhof" (Freiburg Train Station).

Where to stay? With so many places to choose from in this little town, you can't go wrong. Both SmashingConf venues are all centrally located, with countless options nearby. We have listed a few of our favorites on our website.

Obviously, AirBnB has many options as well (and some great affordable options).
