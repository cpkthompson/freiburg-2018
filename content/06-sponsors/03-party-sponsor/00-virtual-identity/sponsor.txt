Title: Virtual Identity AG

----

Text: 

Virtual Identity is a German digital marketing agency that connects brands and people through digital marketing and social media campaigns. Founded in 1995, it has over 180 employees working on creative online marketing campaigns, market research, consulting, performance analysis, and branding. Their customers include Siemens, Lufthansa, Mc Donalds, Allianz, Roche and Infineon.
