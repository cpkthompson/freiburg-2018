Title: Haufe Group

----

Text: 

The Haufe Group is a German-based publisher which has set its reputation as a leading publisher of looseleaf (legal) publications. Over the course of the last years, the family-owned enterprise has been transformed into a provider of digital training and professional development offerings. 

From 1990, the Haufe Group has grown organically from €50m annual revenues to more than €300m, while the number of employees has risen from 330 in 1990 to more than 1,500 today. 95% of its sales is coming from digital products.
