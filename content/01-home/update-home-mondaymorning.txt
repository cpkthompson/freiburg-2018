<h2>Quick Schedule</h2>

<h3>Monday, September 10th</h3>
Kicking off the conference at 9:30AM! Snacks and coffee provided throughout the day. We also have <strong>charging stations</strong> in every other row in the main hall.

The last talk will finish at 6PM, and the <strong>party will start around 8PM in the same venue</strong>, <a href="http://smashed.by/party">Historisches Kaufhaus</a>. What to expect? Lasers! Along with Seb Lee-Delisle's talk <a href="/speakers/seb-lee-delisle">Hack to the Future</a>. Please <strong>don’t forget your badge!</strong>

<h3>Transportation from Basel Airport to Freiburg</h3>
<p>You can take the <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Basel+EuroAirport-Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29&rideDate=09.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=8148&departureStation=&arrivalCity=92&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">Airport Bus</a> from Basel Airport to Freiburg and back, for €20 for one trip. The bus departs once in an hour (check the <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Basel+EuroAirport-Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29&rideDate=09.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=8148&departureStation=&arrivalCity=92&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">time table</a>). The bus driver won’t accept credit cards, so please make sure to have <strong>€20 in cash </strong> with you or <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Basel+EuroAirport-Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29&rideDate=09.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=8148&departureStation=&arrivalCity=92&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">book your ticket</a> ahead of your trip.</p> 
<p>A <strong>taxi</strong> will be quite pricey (€107) and needs to be <strong>pre-booked</strong> via phone (+49-761-555555) &mdash; make sure to have your Credit Card ready. Might be a good option if you’re traveling with friends or colleagues.</p>
