<h3>Transportation from Freiburg to Basel Airport</h3>
<p>You can take the <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29-Basel+EuroAirport&rideDate=13.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=92&departureStation=&arrivalCity=8148&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">Airport Bus</a> from Basel Airport to Freiburg and back, for €20 for one trip. The bus departs once in an hour (check the <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29-Basel+EuroAirport&rideDate=13.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=92&departureStation=&arrivalCity=8148&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">time table</a>). The bus driver won’t accept credit cards, so please make sure to have <strong>€20 in cash </strong> with you or <a href="https://shop.flixbus.com/search?route=Freiburg+%28i.Br.%29-Basel+EuroAirport&rideDate=13.09.2018&adults=1&children=0&bikes=0&_locale=en&departureCity=92&departureStation=&arrivalCity=8148&arrivalStation=&currency=EUR">book your ticket</a> ahead of your trip.</p> 
<p>A <strong>taxi</strong> will be quite pricey (€107) and can be booked via phone (+49-761-555555).</p>
